All 7 BTS members get $20 mn richer each as their agency starts trading

Each of the seven members of South Korean K-pop group BTS became $20 million richer today after their management label Big Hit Entertainment doubled on their first day of trading. Big Hit Founder Bang Si-hyuk gave each of them 68,385 shares in August. The company is now valued at $9.6 billion, bigger than the other three major K-pop agencies combined.

Shakespeare's first collection of plays sells for record ₹73 crore

A copy of the first collection of plays by William Shakespeare printed in 1623 sold for $9.97 million (₹73 crore) at a Christie's auction in New York on Wednesday, setting a new record for a work of literature. The 'First Folio' containing 36 plays was bought by American private collector Stephan Loewentheil. It was compiled by two of Shakespeare's friends.

PIL filed in SC for removal of Andhra CM over remarks against judge

Two advocates have filed a public interest litigation in the Supreme Court seeking the removal of Andhra Pradesh CM YS Jagan Mohan Reddy for his "scandalising" remarks against Justice NV Ramana, the second senior-most judge of the top court. The PIL, filed by lawyers GS Mani and Pradeep Kumar Yadav, contended that CM Jagan is facing over 20 criminal cases.
